# Full Disclosure Policy Documents — Dasmariñas City

The **Full Disclosure Policy (FDP)** is required under **DILG Memorandum Circular 2013-140** and subsequent issuances. All local government units must publicly post key financial and procurement documents on their official website and physical bulletin boards.

Dasmariñas City posts its FDP documents at the City Hall bulletin board and on the **DILG FDP Portal:** https://fdpp.blgs.gov.ph (search "Dasmariñas City").

---

## Required Documents

### Budget Documents

| Document                         | Description                                             |
| -------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| Annual Investment Plan (AIP)     | Planned programs, projects, and activities for the year |
| Annual Budget                    | Appropriations ordinance and breakdown by department    |
| Supplemental Budget              | Additional appropriations during the year               |
| 20% Development Fund Utilization | Use of the 20% IRA for development projects             |
| Trust Fund Utilization           | Use of funds held in trust for specific purposes        |

### Financial Statements

| Document                               | Description                            |
| -------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| Statement of Receipts and Expenditures | Actual income and spending per quarter |
| Statement of Cash Flow                 | Cash position of the city government   |
| Balance Sheet                          | Assets, liabilities, and equity        |
| Statement of Changes in Equity         | Changes in the city's net assets       |

### Procurement Documents

| Document                         | Description                                           |
| -------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| Annual Procurement Plan (APP)    | All planned procurement activities                    |
| Bid Results — Goods and Services | Results of public bidding for goods/services          |
| Bid Results — Infrastructure     | Results of public bidding for infrastructure projects |
| Contracts Awarded                | All contracts entered into by the city                |

### Other Mandatory Documents

| Document            | Description                                        |
| ------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| Manpower Complement | List of all plantilla and contractual employees    |
| SEF Utilization     | Use of Special Education Fund                      |
| DRRMF Utilization   | Use of Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Fund |
| GAD Budget          | Gender and Development budget utilization          |
