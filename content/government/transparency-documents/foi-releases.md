# Freedom of Information (FOI) Releases — Dasmariñas City

Under **Executive Order No. 2 (2016)**, all executive branch agencies — including local government units — are required to make public documents accessible to any requesting citizen.

---

## How to File an FOI Request

### Option 1 — Online

File your request at the national FOI portal:
**https://www.foi.gov.ph** — select "Dasmariñas City" as the agency

### Option 2 — In Person

## Response Timeline

| Step                          | Timeline                                       |
| ----------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| Acknowledgement of request    | Within 3 working days                          |
| Initial response / disclosure | Within 15 working days (extendable by 10 days) |
| Appeal period (if denied)     | Within 15 days of receiving denial             |

---

## Commonly Requested Documents

- Annual budget and supplemental budgets
- Infrastructure project contracts and bid results
- Business permits and licensing data
- City ordinances and resolutions
- Personnel records and plantilla positions
- Environmental clearances
- SALN of elected officials

---

## Exemptions

The following may be withheld under the FOI rules:

- Information affecting national security or defense
- Personal data protected under the Data Privacy Act
- Documents covered by attorney-client privilege
- Preliminary drafts and internal deliberations
- Information under active criminal investigation
