# Public Sessions — Sangguniang Panlungsod ng Dasmariñas City

The **Sangguniang Panlungsod** holds regular legislative sessions that are open to the public. Residents are encouraged to attend, observe, and participate through proper channels.

---

## Session Schedule

| Session Type    | Frequency                                              | Location                           |
| --------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------- |
| Regular Session | Twice a month (as scheduled)                           | Session Hall, Dasmariñas City Hall |
| Special Session | As needed (called by the Mayor or majority of members) | Session Hall                       |
| Public Hearing  | Per committee, before major ordinances                 | City Hall or designated venue      |

For the current session calendar, contact the Sangguniang Panlungsod Secretariat.

---

## How to Attend

1. Bring a valid government-issued ID
2. Sign the attendance log at the entrance
3. Observe proper decorum — no disruptions during deliberations
4. Members of the public may be given time to speak during public hearings

---

## How to Submit a Petition or Position Paper

Residents, organizations, and businesses may submit written petitions or position papers to the Sangguniang Panlungsod:

1. Address your letter to the **Honorable Members of the Sangguniang Panlungsod**
2. Submit to the **Sangguniang Panlungsod Secretariat** at City Hall
3. The Secretariat will assign it to the appropriate committee
4. You may be invited to present during the committee's public hearing
