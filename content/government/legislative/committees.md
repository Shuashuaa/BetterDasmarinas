# Standing Committees — Sangguniang Panlungsod ng Dasmariñas City

The Sangguniang Panlungsod operates through standing committees that review, deliberate, and recommend legislation in their respective areas.

---

## Standing Committees

| Committee                             | Jurisdiction                                                                 |
| ------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Appropriations**                    | City budget, supplemental appropriations, financial allocations              |
| **Laws and Ordinances**               | Review and drafting of city ordinances, legal matters                        |
| **Health and Sanitation**             | Public health programs, sanitation, City Health Office services              |
| **Environment and Natural Resources** | Environmental protection, tree planting, quarrying, waste management (CENRO) |
| **Education**                         | School programs, scholarships, day care, LGU education support (SEF)         |
| **Public Works and Infrastructure**   | Roads, bridges, drainage, public buildings, City Engineering Office matters  |
| **Peace and Order**                   | Public safety, BPSO, barangay peacekeeping, CDRRMO coordination              |
| **Ways and Means**                    | Revenue-generating measures, local taxation                                  |
| **Trade and Commerce**                | Business permits (BPLO), market operations, livelihood programs (PESO)       |
| **Social Services**                   | Senior citizens, PWD, solo parents, relief assistance (CSWDO)                |
| **Agriculture and Fisheries**         | Farming support, veterinary services, fisherfolk programs                    |
| **Housing and Land Use**              | Socialized housing, relocation, zoning, land use planning (CPDO)             |
| **Tourism**                           | Tourism promotion, Paru-Paro Festival, city events                           |

---

## How the Committee Process Works

1. A proposed ordinance or resolution is filed by a councilor or the Mayor
2. The matter is referred to the relevant standing committee
3. The committee holds a public hearing (mandatory for legislation affecting the general public)
4. The committee prepares a report and recommendation
5. The report is submitted to the full council for deliberation and vote
