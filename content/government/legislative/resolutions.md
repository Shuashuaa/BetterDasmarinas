# City Council Resolutions — Dasmariñas City

Resolutions are official declarations by the **Sangguniang Panlungsod ng Dasmariñas City**. Unlike ordinances, they do not carry the force of law but authorize specific actions, express the council's position, or convey formal requests to other government bodies.

---

## Types of Resolutions

| Type                          | Purpose                                                                   |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Authorization Resolutions** | Authorize the Mayor to enter contracts, apply for loans, accept donations |
| **Appropriation Resolutions** | Authorize specific expenditures from the city's funds                     |
| **Commendation Resolutions**  | Recognize individuals, organizations, or achievements                     |
| **Request Resolutions**       | Formally request action from national agencies (DPWH, DepEd, DOH, etc.)   |
| **Position Resolutions**      | Express the city council's stance on legislative or policy matters        |
| **Emergency Resolutions**     | Authorize emergency measures during calamities or disasters               |

---

## How Resolutions Are Passed

1. **Filing** — Councilor files the proposed resolution
2. **Committee referral** (if needed) — For complex matters
3. **Full council deliberation** — Discussion and amendments
4. **Vote** — Majority vote required
5. **Signing** — Signed by the Vice Mayor (presiding officer)
6. **Transmittal** — Sent to the Mayor if executive action is required
