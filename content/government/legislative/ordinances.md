# City Ordinances — Dasmariñas City

City ordinances are local laws enacted by the **Sangguniang Panlungsod ng Dasmariñas City**. They carry the force of law within the city and take effect after the Mayor's signature and proper publication.

---

## How Ordinances Are Passed

1. **Filing** — A councilor, the Mayor, or a citizens' group files a proposed ordinance
2. **Committee referral** — Referred to the appropriate standing committee
3. **Public hearing** — Mandatory for ordinances affecting the general public
4. **Committee report** — Submitted to the full council
5. **First and second readings** — Full council deliberates and votes
6. **Approval** — Requires majority vote; signed by the Vice Mayor (presiding officer)
7. **Mayor's action** — Mayor approves or vetoes within 15 days
8. **Effectivity** — Takes effect 10 days after posting in public places
