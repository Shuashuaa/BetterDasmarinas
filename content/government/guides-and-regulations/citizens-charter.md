# Citizen's Charter — Dasmariñas City

The **Citizen's Charter** is a document required under **Republic Act 11032** (Ease of Doing Business and Efficient Government Service Delivery Act of 2018) and **Republic Act 9485** (Anti-Red Tape Act of 2007).

It sets the **service standards** that residents can expect from every city government office, including maximum processing times and required documents.

---

## Anti-Red Tape Processing Times (RA 11032)

| Transaction Type     | Maximum Processing Time            |
| -------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| **Simple**           | 3 working days                     |
| **Complex**          | 7 working days (extendable to 20)  |
| **Highly Technical** | 20 working days (extendable to 45) |

## Key Services and Standards

- **New Business Permit** — submit requirements at BPLO window; processing within 3–7 working days
- **Business Permit Renewal** — available January of each year; processed within 3 days
- Phone: 0977 169 4323 | Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/p/BPLO-Dasmari%C3%B1as-100070048426539/

- Birth, Marriage, and Death certificate requests

## File a Complaint

If a city office fails to meet the service standards in the Citizen's Charter, you may file a complaint at:

- **Office of the Mayor** — 0995 068 4326
- **Anti-Red Tape Authority (ARTA):** https://arta.gov.ph
- **Civil Service Commission (CSC):** https://csc.gov.ph
- **FOI Portal:** https://www.foi.gov.ph
