# Annual Accomplishment Report — City of Dasmariñas

The **Annual Accomplishment Report** summarizes the programs, projects, and activities (PPAs) completed by the City Government of Dasmariñas during the fiscal year. It is a key accountability document that shows residents how their tax money was spent and what was delivered.

---

## Where to Find the Annual Report

Annual reports are submitted to the **Sangguniang Panlungsod** and published as required under the Local Government Code:

- **City Hall:** Office of the City Administrator, New Socioeconomic Building, Brgy. Burol Main, Dasmariñas, Cavite
- **DILG FDP Portal:** https://fdpp.blgs.gov.ph

---

## Key Program Areas Reported

### 1. Infrastructure and Public Works

Projects typically reported include:

- Road construction, rehabilitation, and concreting (barangay and city roads)
- Drainage and flood control improvements
- Public market upgrades and maintenance
- City hall and public building improvements
- Sports and recreational facilities

### 2. Health Services

- Number of patients served at the Dasmariñas District Hospital and City Health Office
- Vaccination and immunization coverage across 75 barangays
- Maternal and child health program outcomes
- Medical missions and outreach activities

### 3. Social Welfare (CSWDO)

- Senior citizen benefits distributed
- PWD assistance provided
- Solo parent support
- 4Ps (Pantawid Pamilyang Pilipino Program) updates
- Emergency assistance (food packs, cash aid)

### 4. Education (DepEd and SEF)

- Scholarship recipients (city-funded)
- School buildings constructed or repaired (SEF-funded)
- Literacy programs and alternative learning

### 5. Economic Development

- Business permit data (new and renewed)
- Ease of Doing Business improvements
- Livelihood training and assistance
- Dasmariñas CMCI competitiveness ranking updates

### 6. Disaster Risk Reduction (CDRRMO)

- Incidents responded to across 75 barangays
- Evacuations conducted
- Early warning activities
- DRRM Fund utilization

### 7. Environment (CENRO)

- Tree planting activities
- Solid waste management metrics
- Environmental enforcement actions

### 8. Paru-Paro Festival and Cultural Programs

- Annual Paru-Paro Festival activities (November founding anniversary)
- Mass Wedding Program outcomes (held every February 14)
- Cultural heritage preservation activities

## State of the City Address (SOCA)

The Mayor delivers an annual **State of the City Address (SOCA)** to the Sangguniang Panlungsod, which serves as a public summary of the city government's accomplishments and plans. SOCA transcripts and videos are typically available at:

- Official Dasmariñas City Facebook Page
