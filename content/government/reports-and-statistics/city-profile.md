# City Profile and Statistics — City of Dasmariñas

## Overview

**Dasmariñas City** is an **independent component city** in the **Province of Cavite**, Region IV-A (CALABARZON), Philippines. It is the **most populous city in Cavite** and one of the fastest-growing urban centers in the region, serving as a major residential and commercial hub south of Metro Manila.

## Economy

Dasmariñas City's economy is driven by a mix of industry, retail, and services:

- **Manufacturing and Industry** — Home to numerous PEZA-registered economic zones and light industrial establishments
- **Retail and Commerce** — Major commercial corridors along Aguinaldo Highway and Emilio Aguinaldo Highway support thousands of businesses
- **Real Estate** — One of the largest residential subdivision developments outside Metro Manila
- **Healthcare** — Several private hospitals and clinics serve the city and neighboring municipalities
- **Education** — Home to De La Salle University – Dasmariñas (DLSU-D) and numerous higher education institutions

### Major Landmarks and Institutions

- De La Salle University – Dasmariñas (DLSU-D)
- Dasmariñas City Hall (New Socioeconomic Building, Brgy. Burol Main)
- SM City Dasmariñas and Robinsons Place Dasmariñas
- Dasmariñas District Hospital
- Dasmariñas National High School
- Cavite State University – Carmona Campus (nearby)
