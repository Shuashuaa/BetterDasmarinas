# Apply for Socialized Housing or Relocation Projects

Dasmariñas City implements socialized housing programs for qualified low-income residents, in coordination with the National Housing Authority (NHA) and the Housing and Urban Development Coordinating Council (HUDCC).

## Who Can Apply

- Dasmariñas City residents who are informal settlers or living in danger zones (flood-prone areas, easements)
- Low-income households without land ownership
- Families affected by disaster, demolition, or government infrastructure projects
- Priority: families living in flood-prone barangays and along waterway easements

## Requirements

- Valid government-issued IDs (both spouses if married)
- Proof of income (pay slips, barangay certification of livelihood)
- Barangay certification of residency
- Marriage certificate (if applicable)
- Birth certificates of dependents
- Certificate of indigency (if applying for fully subsidized unit)

## How to Apply

1. Visit the **City Social Welfare and Development Office (CSWDO)** or the **City Planning and Development Office (CPDO)**
2. Request and fill out the housing application form
3. Submit complete requirements
4. Undergo home visit and income verification
5. Wait for qualification assessment and unit assignment

## Available Programs

- **NHA Resettlement** — for families displaced from danger zones
- **Community Mortgage Program (CMP)** — for organized informal settler communities
- **Dasmariñas City Local Shelter Plan** — check with CPDO for current projects
