# Report Damaged Roads, Bridges, or Drainage

Residents can report damaged public infrastructure such as potholes, broken bridges, clogged drainage, or defective street lights to the appropriate government office for repair.

## What You Can Report

- Potholes and damaged road surfaces
- Broken or collapsed bridges and footbridges
- Clogged or broken drainage canals and waterways
- Defective or missing street lights
- Damaged public sidewalks or footpaths
- Eroded roads or embankments

## How to Report

### Option 1 — Visit the Office

**City Engineering Office**
New Socioeconomic Building, Brgy. Burol Main, Dasmariñas, Cavite 4114
Phone: 0995 068 4326

### Option 2 — Report to Your Barangay

Barangay officials can escalate infrastructure concerns to the City Engineering Office.

### Option 3 — National Roads

For national roads passing through Dasmariñas (e.g., Emilio Aguinaldo Highway, Governor's Drive), report to:
**DPWH Cavite 1st or 2nd District Engineering Office**

### Option 4 — Online

Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/officeofthecitymayordc

## Information to Include

- Exact location (barangay, street name, landmark)
- Type of damage and severity
- Photo documentation (if available)
- Your name and contact number (for follow-up)

## Response Time

- **Emergency (bridge collapse, major road hazard):** Immediate response
- **Regular repair requests:** 5–15 working days depending on scope

## Follow-Up

Keep a reference of your report date and the office you submitted it to for follow-up inquiries.
