# Request Access to Barangay Water Supply Systems

Dasmariñas City residents without access to private water connections may request connection to barangay-level water supply systems or seek assistance from the Local Water Utilities Administration (LWUA) and the City Government.

## Options for Water Access

### 1. Barangay Waterworks System

Many barangays in Dasmariñas operate their own waterworks. Contact your barangay hall to:

- Apply for a water connection
- Report water supply interruptions
- Request repair of water pipes or meters

### 2. Dasmariñas City Water District

The city water district provides piped water to residential and commercial establishments.

**Dasmariñas City Water District**
For referral, contact the City Hall at 0995 068 4326

### 3. Deep Well / Alternative Sources

For barangays not covered by piped water:

- Request water trucking assistance from the City during dry season
- Apply for DSWD water assistance for indigent families

## How to Apply for Water Connection

1. Visit your barangay hall or the City Hall
2. Fill out the water service application form
3. Submit requirements (valid ID, proof of ownership or lease)
4. Pay connection fees (if applicable)
5. Await installation schedule

## Report Water Issues

- **No water / low pressure:** Contact your barangay waterworks administrator
- **Water quality concerns:** Contact the **City Health Office I** at (046) 416-0809
- **Damaged water main:** Contact the **City Engineering Office** at 0995 068 4326
