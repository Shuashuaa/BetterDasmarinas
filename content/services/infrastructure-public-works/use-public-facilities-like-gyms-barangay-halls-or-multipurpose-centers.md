# Use Public Facilities like Gyms, Barangay Halls, or Multipurpose Centers

Dasmariñas City maintains public facilities that residents can use for community activities, sports, events, and other purposes.

## Available Public Facilities

### Dasmariñas City Sports and Multi-Purpose Facilities

The city maintains covered courts and multi-purpose centers used for:

- Basketball and other sports
- Community assemblies and events
- Training and livelihood activities
- Evacuation centers during disasters

Contact the City Hall for availability and reservation.

### Barangay Halls and Multipurpose Centers

Each of Dasmariñas' 75 barangays has a hall that may be used for:

- Community meetings
- Skills training and seminars
- Livelihood activities
- Voting precincts

Contact your barangay captain to reserve.

### Public Parks and Open Spaces

- **Barangay sports courts** — for basketball and other sports across the city
- **Poblacion public plaza** — for community gatherings

## How to Reserve a Facility

1. Visit the City Hall or the concerned barangay hall
2. Fill out a facility use request form
3. Get approval from the City Mayor's Office or barangay captain
4. Pay any applicable facility fee

## Fees

Some facilities are free for government and community activities. Fees may apply for private events. Check with the **City Mayor's Office** or **City Engineering Office** for current rates.
