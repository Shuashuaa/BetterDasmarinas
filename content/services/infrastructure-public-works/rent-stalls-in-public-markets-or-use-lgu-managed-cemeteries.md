# Rent Stalls in Public Markets or Use LGU-Managed Cemeteries

Dasmariñas City manages public market stalls and the city cemetery. Residents and vendors may apply to rent stalls or use cemetery lots through the City Treasurer's Office.

## Public Market Stalls

### Dasmariñas City Public Market

The public market provides affordable space for vendors selling fresh produce, meat, fish, cooked food, and general merchandise.

**To Apply for a Stall:**

1. Visit the **Dasmariñas City Market Administration Office**
   Phone: 0995 068 4326
2. Fill out the stall application form
3. Submit required documents:
   - Valid government-issued ID
   - Barangay business clearance
   - Health certificate (for food vendors)
   - 2x2 ID photos
4. Pay the stall rental fee at the City Treasurer's Office
5. Receive your stall assignment

## LGU-Managed Cemetery

Dasmariñas City maintains a public cemetery for residents.

**To Apply for a Burial Lot:**

1. Visit the **City Treasurer's Office**
2. Present the death certificate of the deceased
3. Provide proof of residency in Dasmariñas City
4. Pay the cemetery lot fee
5. Receive your lot assignment

**Fees** vary depending on lot type (single, double, or niche). Contact the office for current rates.
