**How to enroll (Elementary)**

1. Go to the **nearest public elementary school** in your barangay during enrollment period (typically May–June, or as announced by DepEd).
2. Bring the following:
   - PSA Birth Certificate (original + photocopy)
   - Report card (for transferees from Grade 1 up)
   - Barangay Certificate of Residency (some schools require this)
   - Immunization records (for Kinder / Grade 1 entrants)
3. Fill out the school's enrollment form and submit to the registrar.

> DepEd assigns students to schools based on **catchment area (barangay)**. Contact the **SDO Dasmariñas** if you are unsure which school covers your address.

**How to enroll (Secondary)**

1. Secure your **Form 138 (Report Card)** from your previous school.
2. Proceed to the **public high school in your assigned catchment area** during enrollment period.
3. For **Senior High School (Grades 11–12)**, choose a track and strand based on available offerings:

| Track                                 | Common Strands                                     |
| ------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| Academic                              | ABM, HUMSS, STEM, GAS                              |
| Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) | Home Economics, ICT, Agri-Fishery, Industrial Arts |
| Sports                                | Sports Track                                       |

4. Submit requirements at the Registrar's Office: PSA Birth Certificate, Form 138, Good Moral Certificate (for transferees), Certificate of Completion (for incoming Grade 7).

> Strand availability varies per school. Confirm with the school or **SDO Dasmariñas** before enrolling.

**Tuition-Free College (RA 10931)**

Under **Republic Act 10931 (Universal Access to Quality Tertiary Education Act)**, Filipino students enrolled in State Universities and Colleges (SUCs) and Local Universities and Colleges (LUCs) may qualify for **free tuition and miscellaneous fees**. The **Kolehiyo ng Lungsod ng Dasmariñas (KLD)** is the city's own LUC.

Apply through your chosen school's registrar or via the **UniFAST portal**. Tuition-free benefits require maintaining satisfactory academic standing — check your school's specific conditions.
