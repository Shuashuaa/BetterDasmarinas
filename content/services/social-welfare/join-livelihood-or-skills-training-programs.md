# Join Livelihood or Skills Training Programs

Dasmariñas City offers livelihood and skills training programs through the City Social Welfare and Development Office (CSWDO), the Public Employment Service Office (PESO), and in partnership with TESDA and DOLE.

---

## Available Programs

### TESDA Skills Training

Free short-term technical-vocational courses including:

- Food processing and culinary arts
- Housekeeping and hotel/hospitality services
- Driving (NCII)
- Electrical installation and maintenance
- Dressmaking and tailoring
- Computer hardware servicing
- Bread and pastry production

**TESDA Cavite Provincial Office:** https://tesda.gov.ph

### DOLE Livelihood Programs

- **TUPAD (Tulong Panghanapbuhay sa Ating Disadvantaged/Displaced Workers)** — emergency employment for displaced workers
- **KABUHAYAN** — starter kits and capital assistance for livelihood projects

### City-Level Programs (CSWDO)

- Handicraft and product-making training
- Pastry and baking training
- Urban gardening and container farming
- Sewing and garment-making
- Beauty care and nail technology

### PESO Employment Assistance

The Public Employment Service Office (PESO) of Dasmariñas City connects job seekers with local employers, job fairs, and government employment programs. Given Dasmariñas' large number of manufacturing and commercial establishments, PESO regularly coordinates job placements in the industrial and retail sectors.

---

## Who Can Join

- Unemployed or underemployed Dasmariñas City residents
- Displaced workers
- Women, PWDs, senior citizens (special programs available)
- Out-of-school youth

---

## How to Enroll

1. Visit the **CSWDO** or **PESO** at the City Hall
2. Ask about upcoming training schedules and available slots
3. Submit your application with a valid ID and barangay certification of residency
4. Attend the training on the scheduled dates
