# Get Family and Community Welfare Support

The City Social Welfare and Development Office (CSWDO) of Dasmariñas City provides family and community welfare services to residents in need of social assistance, counseling, and protection.

---

## Services Available

### Family Welfare

- **Family counseling** — for couples, parent-child, and family conflicts
- **Crisis intervention** — for families in acute distress (violence, abandonment)
- **Referral services** — to appropriate government agencies and NGOs

### Child Welfare

- **Child protection** — intervention for abused, neglected, or exploited children
- **Supplementary feeding** — for malnourished children in coordination with the City Health Office
- **Day care services** — enrollment assistance for children 3–5 years old across Dasmariñas' 75 barangays
- **4Ps (Pantawid Pamilyang Pilipino Program)** — conditional cash transfers for qualified households

### Women's Welfare

- **Violence against women (VAW) assistance** — safe referral and protection
- **Barangay VAW Desk** — available at each of the 75 barangay halls in Dasmariñas
- **Women's livelihood assistance** — skills training and starter kits

### Youth Welfare

- **Juvenile justice intervention** — for youth in conflict with the law
- **Bahay Pag-asa** referral for youth offenders
- **Youth leadership and development programs** — coordinated with SK (Sangguniang Kabataan)
- **Paru-Paro Festival youth activities** — cultural and civic engagement every November

### Indigent Assistance

- **Medical assistance** — for hospitalization and medicines (subject to availability)
- **Educational assistance** — for students in extreme need
- **Burial assistance** — for indigent families who lost a member

---

## Requirements

Requirements vary per program. Bring a valid ID and barangay certificate of residency as a starting point. The CSWDO staff will guide you on what additional documents are needed.
