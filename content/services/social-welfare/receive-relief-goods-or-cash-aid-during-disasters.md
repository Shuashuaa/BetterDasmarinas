# Receive Relief Goods or Cash Aid During Disasters

During calamities, typhoons, flooding, or other disasters, Dasmariñas City provides relief assistance to affected families through the City Social Welfare and Development Office (CSWDO) and the City Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Office (CDRRMO).

---

## Types of Assistance Available

- **Food packs** — rice, canned goods, instant noodles, and other basic food items
- **Non-food items** — blankets, hygiene kits, drinking water
- **Cash aid** — emergency cash assistance for severely affected families
- **Medical assistance** — referral to the City Health Office or Dasmariñas District Hospital

---

## Who Can Receive Assistance

- Dasmariñas City residents whose homes or livelihoods were directly affected by a disaster
- Families who evacuated to official evacuation centers
- Priority: those with no other means of support

---

## How to Receive Relief

### During Evacuation

1. Go to the nearest designated evacuation center in your barangay
2. Register at the CSWDO/CDRRMO registration desk
3. Receive your family's relief pack

### After the Disaster (Home-Based)

1. Report to your **barangay hall** for registration
2. Barangay submits the list to CSWDO
3. Relief distribution is coordinated per barangay across the city's 75 barangays

---

## DSWD Assistance

For large-scale disasters, the **Department of Social Welfare and Development (DSWD)** may also provide assistance. The CSWDO will coordinate on your behalf.
