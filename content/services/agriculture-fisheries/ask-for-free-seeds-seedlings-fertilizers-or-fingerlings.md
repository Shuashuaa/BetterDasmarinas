# Ask for Free Seeds, Seedlings, Fertilizers, or Fingerlings

The Dasmariñas City Agriculture Office distributes free agricultural inputs to registered farmers and fisherfolk to support local food production and livelihood, particularly in the city's farming communities in barangays like Langkaan, Paliparan, and Salawag.

## What is Available

- **Seeds** — vegetable seeds (ampalaya, pechay, sitaw, kamote, etc.)
- **Seedlings** — fruit-bearing tree seedlings (calamansi, banana, papaya)
- **Fertilizers** — organic and inorganic fertilizers
- **Fingerlings** — tilapia and other freshwater fish fingerlings for backyard aquaculture

## Who Can Apply

- Registered farmers and fisherfolk in Dasmariñas City
- Residents with backyard gardens or small farm lots
- Organized farmer/fisherfolk groups and cooperatives

## Requirements

- Valid government-issued ID
- Proof of residence in Dasmariñas City
- Farm lot or garden (barangay certification may be required)
- Registration with the City Agriculture Office (for first-time applicants)

## How to Apply

1. Visit the City Agriculture Office at the New Socioeconomic Building, Brgy. Burol Main
2. Fill out the application form
3. Submit requirements for evaluation
4. Receive your agricultural inputs on the scheduled distribution date

Distributions are done quarterly or during planting seasons. Watch for announcements at your barangay hall or the City Agriculture Office.
