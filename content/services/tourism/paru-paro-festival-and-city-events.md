# Paru-Paro Festival & City Events in Dasmariñas

Dasmariñas City celebrates its culture, history, and community through a calendar of signature events. The most famous is the **Paru-Paro Festival**, held annually every November.

---

## Paru-Paro Festival

The **Paru-Paro Festival** (Butterfly Festival) is the signature annual celebration of **Dasmariñas City**, held every **November** to mark the city's **founding anniversary** (cityhood was granted on November 26, 2009).

### What to Expect

- **Street dancing and float parade** — colorful costumes inspired by butterflies and local culture
- **Cultural performances** — showcasing the arts and traditions of Dasmariñeños
- **Food fair** — local delicacies, street food, and regional dishes
- **Civic programs and award ceremonies**
- **Trade fairs and business expos**
- **Sports events and community activities**

### History

The festival was launched on **November 26, 2011**, marking the second anniversary of Dasmariñas' cityhood. Since then, it has grown into one of the most celebrated festivals in Cavite Province, drawing visitors from across the region.

### When & Where

- **When:** Every November (Cityhood Anniversary Week)
- **Where:** City Hall grounds and major streets of Dasmariñas City
- **Duration:** Typically a full week of events

---

## Mass Wedding Program

The **City of Dasmariñas** holds an annual **Mass Wedding** every **February 14 (Valentine's Day)**, officiated by the city government to help couples formalize their union at no cost.

- **Why it matters:** This is the **largest mass wedding in Cavite Province**
- **Who can join:** Qualified residents of Dasmariñas City (check requirements at City Hall)
- **Contact:** Office of the City Civil Registrar, City Hall, Dasmariñas City

---

## Araw ng Dasmariñas (City Foundation Day)

**Araw ng Dasmariñas** (Day of Dasmariñas) is celebrated on **November 26** each year, commemorating the signing of Republic Act 9723 in 2009, which officially converted the Municipality of Dasmariñas into the **City of Dasmariñas**.

Events typically include:

- Official program at City Hall
- Cultural shows and performances
- Recognition of outstanding Dasmariñeños
- The grand parade and Paru-Paro Festival climax

---

## Religious Festivals

### Feast of the Immaculate Conception

Celebrated on **December 8**, honoring the patron saint of the Immaculate Conception Parish Church in Poblacion. Features a solemn Mass, procession, and community activities.

### Semana Santa (Holy Week)

Religious processions and Stations of the Cross are held in various barangays during Holy Week, reflecting the deep Catholic faith of the community.
