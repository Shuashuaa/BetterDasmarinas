# Explore Tourist Spots & Landmarks in Dasmariñas City

Dasmariñas City offers a rich mix of historical heritage, natural parks, vibrant commercial centers, and community spaces. Here are the key attractions to explore.

---

## Historical & Heritage Sites

### Immaculate Conception Parish Church

One of the most significant historical landmarks in Dasmariñas, the **Immaculate Conception Parish Church** dates back to the Spanish colonial era. Its architecture blends Baroque and Neoclassical styles, featuring intricate altarpieces and beautiful religious artwork. The church is located at the old town center and remains an active place of worship and a popular destination for heritage tourism.

- **Location:** Poblacion, Dasmariñas City, Cavite
- **Open:** Daily during mass schedules

---

### Battle of Perez Dasmariñas Marker

A historical marker commemorating a pivotal event during the **Philippine Revolution**, where Filipino patriots fought against Spanish colonial forces. The marker is a reminder of the city's deep roots in the Philippine independence movement.

- **Location:** Dasmariñas City town center

---

## Parks & Recreation

### Kadiwa Park

One of the most popular public parks in Dasmariñas, **Kadiwa Park** is a beloved recreational and cultural hub featuring:

- Mini-zoo
- Children's playgrounds
- Picnic areas and open lawns
- Walking and jogging paths

A great destination for families, students, and fitness enthusiasts.

- **Location:** Dasmariñas City proper

---

### Dasmariñas Bagong Bayan (DBB) Park

The **Dasmariñas Bagong Bayan** is one of the largest resettlement communities in the Philippines, home to community parks, open spaces, and barangay facilities. The DBB park areas provide green spaces for families, basketball courts, and community gathering places.

- **Location:** Dasmariñas Bagong Bayan, Dasmariñas City

---

## Shopping & Commercial Areas

### SM City Dasmariñas

One of the largest shopping malls in Cavite, **SM City Dasmariñas** offers:

- Retail shopping across multiple floors
- Restaurants and food court
- Cinema complex
- Supermarket and department store
- Regular events and promotions

- **Location:** Burol II, Dasmariñas City, Cavite

---

### Robinsons Place Dasmariñas

Another major commercial hub offering shopping, dining, and entertainment options for Dasmariñeños and visitors alike.

- **Location:** Governor's Drive, Dasmariñas City, Cavite

---

## Universities & Educational Landmarks

Dasmariñas is home to several major universities, making it a significant educational hub in CALABARZON:

- **De La Salle University — Dasmariñas (DLSU-D)** — one of the top universities in Cavite
- **Emilio Aguinaldo College — Cavite Campus**
- **Adventist University of the Philippines** — located nearby in Silang

These campuses attract thousands of students from across the region and add to the city's vibrant, young population.

---

## Industrial Landmarks

As the **Industrial Giant of CALABARZON**, Dasmariñas hosts major industrial estates that drive the province's economy:

- **First Cavite Industrial Estate (FCIE)** — Barangay Langkaan
- **Dasma-Technopark** — Paliparan I
- **Molave Compound** — Paliparan II

These industrial zones are key drivers of employment and economic growth in the region.

---

## Tips for Visitors

- Most attractions are accessible via **jeepney, tricycle, or ride-sharing apps** from the city center
- The best time to visit is during the **dry season (November–April)**
- During the **Paru-Paro Festival (November)**, the city comes alive with cultural events and street activities
- For inquiries, contact the **City Tourism Office** at City Hall, Dasmariñas City, Cavite 4114
