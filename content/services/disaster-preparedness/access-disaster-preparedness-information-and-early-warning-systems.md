# Access Disaster Preparedness Information and Early Warning Systems

Stay informed and prepared through Dasmariñas City's disaster risk reduction programs and early warning systems managed by the CDRRMO.

## Early Warning Systems Available

### Earthquake Monitoring

- **PHIVOLCS** provides 24/7 earthquake and fault monitoring
- Dasmariñas City is near the **West Valley Fault System** — follow all PHIVOLCS advisories

### Weather Warnings

- **PAGASA** issues typhoon signals and rainfall warnings
- Dasmariñas is prone to heavy rains and flooding — monitor Signal No. advisories and Rainfall Warning levels

### Flood Monitoring

- **EFCOS (Effective Flood Control Operation System)** — national flood monitoring system
- Monitor your barangay's flood-prone areas and heed early evacuation calls from CDRRMO

### Community Warning Systems

- Barangay sirens and public address systems across all 75 barangays
- CDRRMO SMS alert registration (ask at your barangay hall)

## Preparedness Materials

Request free copies from CDRRMO or your barangay:

- **Family Disaster Preparedness Guide**
- **Evacuation route maps**
- **Go-Bag checklist**
- **Flood and earthquake hazard maps**

## How to Register for Alerts

1. Visit your barangay hall
2. Register your mobile number for SMS alerts
3. You will receive notifications during disaster warnings

## Build Your Go-Bag

Essential items to prepare in advance:

- Water (at least 3 days' supply per person)
- Non-perishable food
- Flashlight and extra batteries
- First aid kit and medicines
- Important documents (IDs, insurance, land titles) in waterproof bag
- Cash in small bills
- Change of clothes and blanket
