## Key Hazards in Dasmariñas

### Flooding

Several barangays in Dasmariñas are flood-prone, particularly those near the Ylang-Ylang River and other drainage waterways. During heavy rains:

- Monitor PAGASA rainfall advisories
- Evacuate early if your barangay is under flood warning
- Do not cross flooded roads or bridges

### Earthquakes

Dasmariñas is within the influence zone of the **West Valley Fault System**. During an earthquake:

- **Drop, Cover, Hold** — get under a sturdy table or cover your head
- Stay away from windows, shelves, and tall furniture
- After shaking stops, evacuate calmly and check for hazards

### Typhoons

- Follow PAGASA typhoon signal advisories
- Secure loose objects outside your home
- Prepare a Go Bag and know your nearest evacuation center

## Evacuation Routes

- Main evacuation routes use Aguinaldo Highway and Emilio Aguinaldo Highway going north toward Imus or Bacoor
- Barangay-level routes are posted at each barangay hall
