# Join Disaster Drills and Evacuation Planning

The City Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Office (CDRRMO) of Dasmariñas City conducts regular disaster drills and community evacuation planning activities to prepare residents for emergencies across the city's 75 barangays.

## Types of Drills Conducted

- **Earthquake drill** — "Duck, Cover, Hold" and evacuation procedures (West Valley Fault proximity)
- **Flood drill** — evacuation for flood-prone barangays along rivers and low-lying areas
- **Fire drill** — evacuation and fire suppression basics
- **Typhoon preparedness drill** — shelter-in-place and evacuation procedures

## How to Participate

1. Watch for announcements from your barangay hall or CDRRMO
2. Schools, offices, and community groups may request a drill by contacting CDRRMO
3. Attend the scheduled drill at your workplace, school, or community

## Evacuation Planning

Your barangay has a designated **Evacuation Center**. Know yours before a disaster strikes. Evacuation maps are posted at barangay halls.

**Key Evacuation Centers in Dasmariñas:**

- Designated covered courts and multi-purpose centers per barangay
- Dasmariñas City Hall grounds
- Public school buildings designated as evacuation sites

## Tips for Residents

- Know your evacuation route and meeting point
- Prepare a "Go Bag" with essentials (water, food, documents, medicines)
- Sign up for the city's early warning SMS alerts at your barangay hall
